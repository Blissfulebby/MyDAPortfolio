cholera Outbreak Stockholm 1835 Analysis

This is an Analysis of Cholera outbreak which occurred in Stockholm, Sweden in late August 1853 leading to About 3% of the population dying within a couple of weeks. Poor parts of the city such as Saint Catherine Parish was struck. Only death records have been analyzed. The data is collected from hand-written church records. The church was responsible for all population registration until around 1870. All data can be found at the National Archive of Sweden. The dataset (Sweden) was translated using Google sheet, which is very effective for large dataset translation. A total of 248 persons died in Catherine Parish Female death was 52% while male death was 48%, More female death was recorded compared to male. Highest death rate was in September and least death rate occurred in December. Older adults had total victim of 103, middle aged adults 80, infants with 26, lastly young adults with 6. .
